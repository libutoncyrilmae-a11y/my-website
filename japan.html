Why I Want To Visit . . .

Shibuya, Japan

I have always dreamed of visiting Japan because of its beautiful balance between tradition and modern life. I want to walk under cherry blossoms, explore peaceful temples, and experience the energy of its bright cities. The culture, the food, and the scenery all feel inspiring to me. Visiting Japan would not just be a trip, but a meaningful experience I would always remember.
