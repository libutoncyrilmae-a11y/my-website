My Bucket List

Experiences and achievements I dream of accomplishing in my lifetime

Grow My Crochet Business

I dream of expanding my crochet business into something more, perhaps starting from booths is a great start. Hopefully with those experiences, I would get to start a yarn business of my own, being a supplier to many costumers.

World Travel with Mom

To create many memories with my mom is enough to fullfil my bucket list. Being able to travel around the world with her by my side is my number one goal.

Own a Small Loft House

A small loft house is the only house I can call a home. A warm, cozy, homy space that I can can my own, It may be small and simple, but it's mine.

Live in China

Living in China is not just a bucket list but a huge dream of mine. When the time comes and I'm financially capabale enough, I hope to settle in China and hopefully get to work there as well.
