My Film TV Shows

My favorite series

Modern Family

Sitcom / Comedy

A mockumentary-style sitcom following three related families and their everyday lives. I love coming back to this series whenever I need a good laugh, it feels like a cozy, funny hug for my evenings.

Created by Christopher Lloyd & Steven Levitan

The Queen's Gambit

Drama / Miniseries

A miniseries about a young chess prodigy rising through the ranks. This show always inspires me when I feel stuck, Beth's journey is thrilling and smart, she inspired me to play chess.

Created by Scott Frank

Kim's Convenience

Sitcom / Comedy

A sitcom about a Korean-Canadian family running a convenience store. Watching this series feels like visiting a warm, funny family I wish I had nearby, it is perfectly cozy and heartwarming.

Created by Ins Choi & Kevin White

3%

Sci-Fi / Thriller

A dystopian sci-fi series where candidates compete for a chance at a better life. This series is perfect for those who seek thrill and mystery, this serie helped me through the pandemic.

Created by Pedro Aguilera

The 100

Sci-Fi / Drama

A post-apocalyptic sci-fi series following 100 juvenile delinquents sent back to Earth. Though the serie is so long, it never gets boring, it gets interesting every episode.

Created by Jason Rothenberg

Dead to Me

Comedy / Drama

Darkly comedic tale of grief, friendship, and secrets. This serie is both funny and twisted, it almost reminds me of the movie Gone Girl.

Created by Liz Feldman
