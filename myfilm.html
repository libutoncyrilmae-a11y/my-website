My Personal Movie Favorites

Some of my all-time favorite films

Interstellar

Genre: Sci-Fi / Adventure

Year: 2014

An epic exploration of space and time, with love and survival at its core. One of the best movies I've ever watched. If you enjoy movies that involves outer space and thrills, this movie is for you.

The Karate Kid

Genre: Action / Drama

Year: 2010

Jaden Smith learns martial arts to overcome challenges and find inner strength. My childhood fave as well, I grew up watching this. It's very inspiring and motivational.

Coraline

Genre: Fantasy / Animation

Year: 2009

A young girl discovers a mysterious parallel world with dark secrets. I grew up watching this and I still do up to this day, it's both fun and mysterious.

Now You See Me

Genre: Mystery / Thriller

Year: 2013

A team of magicians pulls off daring heists while staying ahead of the law. One of the best movies out there to watch with your family.

Oppenheimer

Genre: Biography / Drama

Year: 2023

The story of J. Robert Oppenheimer and the moral dilemmas he faced while developing the atomic bomb. A very educational and interesting movie.
