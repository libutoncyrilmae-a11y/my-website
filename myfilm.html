Some of my all-time favorite films

Interstellar

Year: 2014

The Karate Kid

Genre: Action / Drama

Year: 2010

Jaden Smith learns martial arts to overcome challenges and find inner strength.

Oppenheimer

The story of J. Robert Oppenheimer, the “father of the atomic bomb,” and the moral dilemmas he faced.
