Why I Want To Visit . . .

Barcelona, Spain

The movie "Barcelona" by KathNiel is probably one of the reasons why I want to visit this place. They did not fail to capture the beauty of the country in the film, doing really well in highlighting tourist spots as well as the brief historical stories of certain places. Just from the movie itself, Barcelona seems like a dreamy place, and if I get to visit, I'll feel so lucky to be able to experience it.
